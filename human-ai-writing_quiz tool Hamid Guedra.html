Academic Integrity: Human-AI Contribution Scale

AI-Collaboration Levels (Generated with Gemini Canvas, Hamid Guedra)

The Spectrum of Authorship

Click the spectrum bars for more information

👩‍🎓

100% Human Only

No AI use (except basic proofreading).

💡

75% | 25% Human-Led

Author writes core content; AI provides feedback/minor editing.

🤝

50% | 50% Human-AI Collaboration

AI generates large parts, but human heavily edits and improves. Risky. Could be considered academic misconduct.

🚨

25% | 75% AI-Led

Largely written by AI; Academic misconduct.

🤖

100% AI Only

Copied completely from AI; Academic misconduct.

Goal: Aim for the top two levels (100% or 75%/25%). Risk: The bottom two levels (25%/75% and 100%) constitute academic misconduct.

Inspired by Jim O’Mahony: “My easy to follow guide for attribution” and Ville Arvola’s GenAI hyödyntämisen tasot (presented at ITK2025).
